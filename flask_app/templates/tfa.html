Two-Factor Auth Enrollment

This website requires mandatory 2FA enrollment.

Use a authenticator app (such as DuoMobile) to enroll with us! You will need to use this app to login in the future. Once you've downloaded the app, just use your phone camera and scan the barcode! This will open the authenticator app, which will have a constantly updating authentication 6 digit code that you will use to login!

When you're done, you can go to the Login page!
